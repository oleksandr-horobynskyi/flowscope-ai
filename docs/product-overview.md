# Product Overview

## Project Name

FlowScope AI

## One-Line Description

AI business assistant for small businesses that turns chaotic requests into structured next steps.

## Short Description

FlowScope AI helps small businesses describe a business problem in plain language, clarify the context, identify the task type, and receive a practical scenario for what to do next. The first MVP branch focuses on AI-assisted CV screening and candidate ranking.

## Core Value Proposition

Small businesses often know they have a problem, but not always what to automate first or how to structure the request. FlowScope AI becomes a simple Telegram-first entry point into AI-assisted decision support.

## What Problem It Solves

- unstructured business requests
- delayed decision-making
- too much manual routine
- lack of dedicated HR / ops / automation specialists in SMBs
- slow and inconsistent first-stage recruitment

## Current MVP Branch

The hackathon MVP demonstrates one clear scenario:

- recruitment screening

The assistant:

- accepts a raw hiring request
- clarifies requirements
- routes the case to the recruitment branch
- structures the hiring task
- prepares a recruiter-friendly output

## Why Telegram

- fast entry point for non-technical users
- no heavy onboarding
- natural chat-based input for messy real-world requests
- easy to demo and easy to understand

## Strategic Vision

FlowScope AI is designed as a platform concept, not a single isolated HR tool. Recruitment is the first demonstrated branch, but the same logic can later serve lead processing, operations triage, internal workflow analysis, and other SMB scenarios.
